Amie Albright [ philosophies ]

I am a multi-media artist focusing on conceptual abstractions within the scope(s) of both cultural and physical anthropology, philosophies, politics, sciences, and societal studies/observations to name a few, in hopes to engage others from an empathetic place. I hope to create a space for deep contemplations on worldy issues. A space where new ideas and solutions may flourish.

I have a preference and romance for working with both medium and large format film cameras. There is something so special about creating images at a slow pace, carefully composing every detail. Every photo series is an experiment of light, composition, and chemistry. Developing is both exhilirating and terrifying, as I can never really know what my vision of 12 hours prior truly produced.
